📚 Theoretical Analysis

Optimal Policy
Q-Learning learns the theoretically shortest path hugging the cliff edge (Row 2). SARSA learns a safer detour via the top row, avoiding cliff risk during exploration.
Stability
SARSA's on-policy updates internalize exploration risk, yielding lower variance per episode. Q-Learning's off-policy update ignores ε-greedy exploration, leading to higher variance near the cliff.
Convergence
SARSA converges to a higher average reward (safe path ≈ −24) under ε-greedy. Q-Learning aims for the optimal greedy reward (≈ −13) but suffers during training due to cliff falls.
